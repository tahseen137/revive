## DM Strategy & Best Practices

### When to Send
- **Best times**: Tues-Thurs, 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM (their timezone)
- **Avoid**: Mondays (inbox overload), Fridays (checked out), weekends (invasive)

### Personalization Checklist
Before sending, can you answer YES to these?
- [ ] Used their actual name (not "Hey there")
- [ ] Mentioned their product/company specifically
- [ ] Referenced something recent (tweet, milestone, post)
- [ ] Estimated their MRR/problem size accurately
- [ ] Explained why THIS tool for THEM (not generic pitch)

### Response Rates (Expected)
- **Cold DMs (no prior interaction)**: 5-15% response rate
- **Warm DMs (they engaged with your content)**: 30-50% response rate
- **Referral DMs (mutual connection mentioned)**: 60%+ response rate

### Follow-Up Cadence
- **Day 1**: Send initial DM
- **Day 4**: Soft follow-up if no response ("Hey [Name], just bumping this in case it got buried. No pressure either way!")
- **Day 8**: Final follow-up with value-add ("Saw you posted about [X] â€” thought this article might help: [link]. Also, still happy to set up that free trial if you're curious about Revive.")
- **After Day 8**: Stop. Don't be annoying.

### Red Flags (Don't Send If...)
- They have "No DMs" in bio
- They explicitly say "Don't pitch me"
- They're in a completely unrelated industry (B2C, e-commerce, non-SaaS)
- You can't find ANY way to personalize it
- Your estimate of their MRR is a total guess (makes you look dumb if wrong)

### Conversion Tips
1. **Lead with empathy, not pitch** ("Have you dealt with this?" > "Buy my thing")
2. **Make the ROI obvious** (numbers beat adjectives)
3. **Reduce friction** (free trial, no CC, 3-min setup)
4. **Offer a graceful exit** ("Even if you don't use it, worth checking your Stripe dashboard")
5. **Sound like a human** (use contractions, casual language, emojis sparingly)

### What to Track
- **Sent**: How many DMs per day (don't spam â€” 10-15 max)
- **Opened**: Did they read it? (Twitter shows this)
- **Replied**: What % respond?
- **Booked**: What % want a demo/trial?
- **Converted**: What % become customers?

Aim for:
- 10% reply rate (cold)
- 50% trial signup (of replies)
- 30% conversion (of trials)

If you're below these, tweak your copy or targeting.
