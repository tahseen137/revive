## Executive Summary

SaaS founders searching for failed payment solutions use keywords emphasizing **automation**, **AI/ML optimization**, **revenue maximization**, and **churn reduction**. Long-tail phrases with commercial intent (e.g., "best tool for...", "software to recover...") indicate high buyer readiness.

**Key Finding**: Searches often combine "SaaS" with action verbs like "recover," "reduce," or "automate" to filter for ready-to-deploy tools.
