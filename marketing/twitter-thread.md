## Thread Best Practices

- **Hook in first 100 characters** (mobile preview matters)
- **1 idea per tweet** (easy to read, easy to retweet individual tweets)
- **Numbers > adjectives** ("94% recovery rate" > "really good recovery")
- **End with clear CTA** (link + action verb)
- **Optional engagement tweet** at the end (builds followers, starts convos)

## Posting Strategy

1. **Post at 9-11 AM EST** (highest engagement for B2B SaaS audience)
2. **Pin the thread** to profile for 48 hours
3. **Retweet yourself** 6-8 hours later (different timezone audience)
4. **Reply to comments quickly** (first 2 hours matter most for engagement)
5. **Quote-tweet with results** after 24 hours ("This thread got 50 signups. Here's what I learned...")
