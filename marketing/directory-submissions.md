### Full Description (500-1000 words)

**Recover failed payments. Automatically.**

Revive is the first full-stack payment recovery tool built for indie SaaS founders who refuse to pay revenue share fees.

**The Problem:**
- 9% of MRR is lost to failed payments on average (industry data)
- 73% of that is recoverable with the right system
- Existing tools (ChurnKey, Churn Buster) charge 10-25% revenue share + $250+/mo base fees
- Most tools only support Stripe — leaving Lemon Squeezy, Gumroad, Paddle users behind

**The Solution:**
Revive handles the full recovery stack — intelligent retries, automated dunning emails, and win-back campaigns — for a flat $49/mo. No revenue share. Ever.

**Key Features:**
1. **Intelligent Retry Engine** — Analyzes each decline and schedules retries at the optimal time (not random intervals). 3.2x higher recovery rate vs. blind retry tools.

2. **Automated Dunning Sequences** — Personalized email sequences triggered by payment failure with one-click card update links (no login required). 68% open rate on average.

3. **Automated Win-Back Campaigns** — Sequences that run at 7, 14, and 30 days after cancellation. 12% reactivation rate. Zero manual effort.

4. **Multi-Platform Support** — Works with Stripe, Lemon Squeezy, Gumroad, Paddle, and Polar.sh. One integration, all payment platforms.

5. **Recovery Analytics** — Real-time dashboard showing recovery rate, revenue recovered, email performance, and win-back conversions. Data you can act on.

6. **Secure by Default** — Read-only API access. We never store card data. All payments flow through your payment platform's secure API.

**Pricing Comparison:**
- **Revive**: $49/mo flat
- **ChurnKey**: $250/mo + 10-25% revenue share (=$350-500+/mo at $10K MRR)
- **Churn Buster**: $249/mo
- **Baremetrics**: $204/mo (dashboards only, no automation)

**ROI Examples:**
- At $5K MRR: Recover ~$329/mo (ROI: 6.7x)
- At $10K MRR: Recover ~$657/mo (ROI: 13.4x)
- At $20K MRR: Recover ~$1,314/mo (ROI: 26.8x)

**Setup:** 15 minutes. No code. No engineers. No ongoing maintenance.

**Free Trial:** Free until it recovers at least $49. No credit card required.

**Target Users:** Indie SaaS founders, bootstrapped startups, multi-platform creators using Stripe/Lemon Squeezy/Gumroad/Paddle/Polar.sh
