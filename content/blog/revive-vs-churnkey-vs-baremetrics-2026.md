## What is Churnkey?

Churnkey is a "retention platform" that combines **cancel flows** (deflection tactics when users hit the cancel button) with **payment recovery** (dunning for failed charges).

### How Churnkey Works

1. **Cancel Flows:** When a user clicks "cancel subscription," Churnkey intercepts with offers (pause, discount, downgrade)
2. **Dunning Engine:** Sends email sequences and retries failed payments on a schedule
3. **A/B Testing:** Test different flows and messages to optimize retention
