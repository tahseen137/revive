Recovery Rate Benchmarks

What should you expect from dunning software?

  • No dunning (Stripe Smart Retries only): 20-30% recovery
  • Basic dunning (email only, no optimization): 40-50% recovery
  • Good dunning (smart retries + email sequence): 60-70% recovery
  • Excellent dunning (segmentation + A/B testing + optimization): 70-80% recovery

If you're under 50%, something's broken. Check:

  • Are your emails going to spam?
  • Are you retrying too fast (same day = waste of time)?
  • Is your payment update link actually working?

Common Dunning Mistakes

1. Canceling subscriptions too quickly
Give customers 14 days before canceling. Most want to stay — they just need a nudge.

2. Generic email templates
Personalize. Include the last 4 digits of their card. Make the update link one-click (no login).

3. Retrying the same day for insufficient funds
Wait 3 days. If they didn't have money at 9am, they won't have it at 3pm.

4. Not tracking decline codes
Expired cards need a different email than insufficient funds. Route by decline code, not a one-size-fits-all sequence.

5. Sending emails from a no-reply address
Use a real email address. Some customers will reply asking for help — answer them and you'll recover more.
